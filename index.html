Mission Statement

Our mission statement is to be creative. Creativity is an area in which everyone wants to express themselves. We aim to do this through the realease of Original content in the form of music headed up by our founder Nick Fowler. This includes instrumentals, as well as singles, and albums varrying in genre from rock to hip-hop. We also aim to give our local communities shows in an attempt to give a voice to our local musicians.

Other Ventures

We also aim to release video content in the form of original short sketches, documentaries, podcasts, and short films. Along with audio and written stories varrying from opinion to profile peices. This will be headed by our Vice President Dakota Rowlison. Together our creative minds will design merch for various projects in stylish ways that represent each indivual project. The sky is the limit here at ISP and if you wish to join us a patron link will be provided soon.
