It's Snowin' Productions Vice President Dakota Rowlison

Biography

Dakota Rowlison is our Vice President here at Talent relations and is heading up our Video and multimedia productions. He is currently attending Fort Hays State University with an expected graduation of May 2021 with a Bachelors degree in Informatics with emphasises in Digital Media & Jounalism. Dakota was born in 1996 and is from Salina, Kansas. His biggest inspirations are Quinten Tarrintino, the Cohen Brothers, & Donald Glover.

Dakota's Vision for ISP

Dakota has worked for nummerous production companies such as the Pylon, Salina Public Access, and most recently Tiger Media Network. In his short time working in media he has accumilated awards for news stories, documentaries, and student talk shows from KSPA and KBA. He is proficient behind the camera, behind a production board, directing, and writting stories along with scripted material. Dakota also has a relationship with many surrounding musical acts and is focused on giving them a platform to showcase their talents.
