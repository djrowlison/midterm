It's Snowin' Productions Founder Nick Fowler

Biography

Nick Fowler is the current President & CEO of It's Snowin' Productions. Nick Founded the company in 2013 while attending college. Nick Gradutated with a Bachelors Degree in Communications in 2016 from Kansas Weslyan University in Salina, Kansas. While there he showed in intrest in radio and television production while activley participating in programs like debate and student government.

Nick's Vision for ISP

He was born in 1993 in El Dorado, Kansas and learned to love music at a young age. He started making music at the age of 15 with his biggest influences being Ice Cube, Kanye West, & Elsa from Frozen. Most recently he has worked as a social media rep for many companies in the Tallahasse, Fort Lauderdale, and Witchita areas. Nick also has experience in Music production & Performance as well as Cinema Photagraphy. His vision for ISP is to expand the social, marketing and audio output of the company.
